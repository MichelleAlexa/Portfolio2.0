Velvet Beauty Bar

Web Design + Web Development

Freelance


Goal:

Established a digital presence

Increase traffic on their site to attract new/potential customers to their spalon

Create the overall look, feel and functionality of the site


Role:

Front-End Developer and Web Designer

Involved in all web design stages from concept to final front end implementation

Velvet is a new concept on beauty clinics. They are a spalon in Toluca, Mexico whose treatments are carefully curated for their client needs.

They use state-of-the-art technology, non-surgical treatments and the most advanced techniques to achieve the best results with the least amount of disruption to everyday life.

My goal as lead designer on Velvet's website was establish their digital presence, ensure their brand was ... conveyed digitally. The website showcases their services and allows customers to easily book appointments in advance.
