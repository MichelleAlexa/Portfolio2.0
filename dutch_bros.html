DutchBros Rewards App

Ux/Ui Designer & Branding

Side Project


Goal:

Replace the use of thier punchcards with a digital one.

Create the look, feel and functionality of the App


DutchBros has become one of the country's largest, privately held drive-thru coffee company and my favorite go place for a coffee run!

Their rewards program consits of having a punchcard that keeps track of drinks you've purchased, once you've reached 10 drinks, they reward you by making your 11th drink free.

My idea is to essentialy replace their use of punchcards with the digital version of it. This "rewards" concept app will let you earn, redeem and keep track of your purchases. The idea here is to virtually carry your punchcard with you anywhere.
